About Me

There's just something rewarding about building something from the ground up. It may have just started with a concept but with a little time and hard work, you watch it come to fruition. As it grows piece by piece, it develops into something bigger until that moment where you can finally say " I. MADE. THAT! " This feeling of accomplishment led me to become a passionate software engineer utilizing the ever-expanding list of technologies to build a world where the only limit is my imagination. There is no better feeling than when I'm able to watch my app grow into something more than I could've thought possible while leaving room for more still yet to be seen.

I graduated from San Jose State with a BS degree majoring in economics and minoring in business. Due to COVID-19, my plans for pursuing a career in business post graduation did not go through as planned but that could've been the best thing to happen to me at the time. It was time to try something new...

Can you guess what I ended up doing?

If you guessed Software Development, you are absolutely correct!

I pursued a new career and graduated from App Academy, a highly selective software engineering program where I spent countless hours a week learning and honing my skills in the diferrent technologies and languages of software development. Although it was an extremely challenging hurdle to overcome, it ignited my passion for problem solving and the desire for continuous learning. The only thing in life that is constant is change and the most effective way to deal with change is with lifelong learning which is why I strive to become the most optimal problem solver as a programmer.

When I'm not coding, I'm often looking for something that needs fixing or anything that needs work to be done. My main hobby is working on my project car so that it'll be ready for its next carshow.
